# Privacy Policy
**Last Updated:** April 1, 2026

## 1. Introduction

The cornwallCORE ("the Bot") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our Discord bot.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Automatically Collected Information
When you interact with the Bot, we automatically collect:
- **Discord User ID**: Unique identifier for your Discord account
- **Discord Username**: Your display name on Discord
- **Server Information**: Server and channel IDs where the Bot operates
- **Message Content**: Text of messages you send (for temporary storage and reposting)
- **Command Usage Data**: Information about which commands you use and when

### 2.2 Information You Provide
- **ROBLOX Username**: When using enlistment features
- **User Mentions**: When using commands that reference other users
- **Custom Messages**: When using direct messaging features

### 2.3 Technical Information
- **Timestamps**: When messages are sent and commands are used
- **Message Metadata**: Channel IDs, message IDs for Bot functionality
- **Error Logs**: Technical information for troubleshooting and service improvement

## 3. How We Use Your Information

### 3.1 Core Bot Functionality
- **Command Execution**: Process and respond to your commands
- **User Verification**: Verify ROBLOX accounts for regimental enlistment
- **Message Management**: Store and repost messages as configured
- **Role Management**: Assign and manage Discord roles based on verification

### 3.2 Service Improvement
- **Error Diagnosis**: Analyze logs to fix bugs and improve performance
- **Usage Analytics**: Understand how features are used to optimize the Bot
- **Security Monitoring**: Detect and prevent abuse or unauthorized access

### 3.3 Communication
- **Direct Messages**: Send messages on behalf of authorized users
- **Deployment Notifications**: Send regimental deployment messages
- **System Updates**: Notify users of important Bot changes or maintenance

## 4. Data Storage and Retention

### 4.1 Message Storage
- **Duration**: Messages are stored in memory for maximum 24 hours
- **Purpose**: Solely for the message reposting functionality
- **Location**: Temporary memory storage (not persistent database)
- **Cleanup**: Automatic deletion every hour for messages older than 24 hours

### 4.2 User Data
- **Discord Information**: Stored only as long as necessary for Bot functionality
- **ROBLOX Verification Data**: Retained for regimental roster management
- **Command Logs**: Stored temporarily for debugging and security purposes

### 4.3 Data Minimization
- We collect only the information necessary for Bot functionality
- No unnecessary personal data is collected or retained
- Message content is not permanently stored

## 5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

### 5.1 No Third-Party Sharing
- We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties
- Your data is not used for advertising or commercial purposes
- No data is shared with data brokers or marketing companies

### 5.2 Limited Disclosure
We may share information only in the following circumstances:
- **Legal Requirements**: When required by law, court order, or government regulation
- **Security Purposes**: To protect our systems, users, or prevent fraud
- **Service Providers**: With trusted service providers who assist in Bot operations (under strict confidentiality agreements)

### 5.3 Regimental Management
- ROBLOX verification information may be used by regimental leadership for roster management
- User role assignments are visible to server administrators with appropriate permissions

## 6. Data Security

### 6.1 Technical Measures
- **Encryption**: Data transmission uses Discord's secure connections
- **Access Controls**: Limited access to Bot systems and data
- **Memory Management**: Automatic cleanup of temporary message storage
- **Error Handling**: Secure logging without sensitive information exposure

### 6.2 Administrative Measures
- **Limited Access**: Only authorized administrators can access Bot systems
- **Regular Reviews**: Periodic security audits and data handling reviews
- **Training**: Bot operators trained on privacy and security best practices

## 7. Your Rights and Choices

### 7.1 Data Access
- You can request information about what data we have about you
- Contact Bot administrators through official Discord channels

### 7.2 Data Correction
- If your ROBLOX username or other information is incorrect, contact administrators for updates
- You can update your Discord information through Discord's settings

### 7.3 Message Control
- Messages are automatically deleted from Bot memory after 24 hours
- You can request manual deletion of your stored messages by contacting administrators
- Use Discord's own message deletion features for permanent removal

### 7.4 Bot Usage
- You can stop using the Bot at any time
- You can request removal from regimental systems if applicable

## 8. Message Reposting Privacy

### 8.1 How It Works
- Messages are temporarily stored for automatic reposting functionality
- Reposting occurs randomly in designated channels
- Bot messages and system messages are automatically excluded

### 8.2 Your Control
- Messages are automatically removed from storage after 24 hours
- You can request immediate deletion of your stored messages
- Server administrators can disable the reposting feature

### 8.3 Limitations
- Once reposted, messages become part of Discord's permanent chat history
- The Bot cannot delete messages from Discord's servers
- Original messages remain in Discord regardless of Bot storage

## 9. Children's Privacy

The Bot is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected such information, we will take steps to delete it promptly.

## 10. International Data Transfers

Your information may be processed and stored on servers in different countries. When we transfer your data internationally, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your privacy rights.

## 11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted in this document and updated "Last Updated" date. Significant changes will be announced through official Discord channels.

## 12. Contact Information

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or want to exercise your data rights, please contact the Bot administrators through the official Discord server channels.

## 13. Discord's Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy supplements Discord's Privacy Policy. Discord's collection and use of your data is governed by their own privacy policies, which you should review for complete information about Discord's data practices.

## 14. Data Breach Notification

In the event of a data breach that affects your personal information, we will:
- Promptly assess the nature and scope of the breach
- Take immediate steps to secure our systems
- Notify affected users through appropriate Discord channels
- Cooperate with any regulatory requirements

## 15. Legal Basis for Processing

Our legal basis for processing your information includes:
- **Legitimate Interest**: For Bot functionality and service improvement
- **Contractual Necessity**: When required to provide agreed-upon services
- **Legal Obligation**: When required by applicable laws
